my sketchbook

woman-owned · feral art

Hi! My name is Snow and this is my sketchbook. I have always loved art; creating it, looking at it, collecting it. Admittedly, I stopped creating while I raised my kids. Now they're older, and when my dad passed away a couple years ago, I needed an outlet. I found my ability to create again. And now I'm here, sharing it with whoever needs it.
